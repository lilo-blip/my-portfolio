About Me

Background

In 2016, I enrolled in Machakos University for a Bachelor's Degree in Education(Science), with Mathematics and Physics as my chosen teaching subjects. I had a difficult time choosing a major between Mathematics and Physics, since I was equally interested in both. However, after a lot of deliberation, I finally graduated in April of 2021 with a major in Physics.

After graduation, I ended up working with Deaf Empowerment Kenya, an NGO based in Machakos. Through that and working directly with the local community, I gained a lot ok knowledge on matters Sexual and Reproductive Health, Sexual and Gender-Based Violence, Rape and Post-Rape Emergency Medical Procedures, and Life Skills.

Why Programming?

I have always been interested with how things work and why they work the way that they do (I'm beginning to see why I chose Physics over Mathematics). "What makes the text 'blue'? How come when I click a 'blue-highlighted' text it takes me to a different page or section of a page? How did this picture get here?". Therefore when someone suggested I sign-up for a Software Development class, I did not hesitate.
